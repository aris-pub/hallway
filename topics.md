## arXiv Independence (March 2026)

arXiv is becoming an independent nonprofit (July 1, 2026) after 35 years under
Cornell. Simons Foundation backing, hiring a CEO, $6M budget.

Angle: What does it mean when the dominant preprint server becomes a standalone
nonprofit? Not a Press pitch — an honest analysis of governance, sustainability,
and what researchers should watch for. Written from the perspective of a
researcher who builds infrastructure.

Questions to explore:
- Does independence let arXiv evolve faster, or does nonprofit governance add its
  own inertia?
- What should researchers watch for in the CEO hire? (Tech-forward vs.
  governance-focused signals very different futures.)
- The community's OpenAI anxiety — is it warranted, or is arXiv's incentive
  structure fundamentally different?
- What does arXiv's $6M/27-staff reality tell us about the cost of maintaining
  scholarly infrastructure?
- Will an independent arXiv ever expand beyond PDF?

The same week, a philosophy PhD student discovered their journal review was likely written entirely by AI. A problem previously confined to computer science has reached the humanities.

AI peer review is arriving from two directions at once: sanctioned and unsanctioned. This week's edition also covers CAS deploying a research AI platform across 50+ Chinese institutes and the NEJM's first retraction since 2020 for AI-manipulated imagery.
